Acid学校 第一話
Acid Gakkou is a new dance party focused on hard-edged hardware sounds grounded in a multi-genre approach. Acid Gakkou is focused on bringing the most exciting upcoming Tokyo local DJs to each event with an emphasis on creating a cohesive and highly danceable musical journey from start to finish.
