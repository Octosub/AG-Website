ACID学校

Acid Gakkou is a dance party focused on hard-edged hardware sounds grounded in a multi-genre approach. Every two months we’re bringing the most exciting DJs from Tokyo and beyond to our party, with an emphasis on selectors who are representing fresh, raw, underground sounds. Timetables are arranged to create a cohesive and highly danceable musical journey.

Residents

Mark Shephard

Acid Gakko boss Mark Shephard is bringing his signature hardware live set back to Traffic for the first time since Marchs packed party with Switch. With a simple setup of drum machines and synthesizers, Marks hardware live takes angular twists through hard-edged acid, breaks, and trance.

Noah Taiga aka Roller

Roller, aka Noah Taiga is a co-organizer and resident DJ of Acid Gakkou. His style is heavily influenced by his upbringing in Germany with a preference for dark moody grooves and trippy rhythms. Noah is also regularly guest DJ-ing at parties around Tokyo, particularly in the party hotspot neighborhoods of Sangen-Jaya and Shibuya.
